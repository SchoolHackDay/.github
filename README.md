# SchoolHackDay 🎓💡

Welcome to **SchoolHackDay** — a community where students build, share, and learn together through hackathon projects!

## About

SchoolHackDay is an initiative that brings students together to collaborate, create, and showcase their ideas through hands-on hackathon events. This organisation hosts the projects built by students during SchoolHackDay events.

## Projects

All repositories in this organisation are student-created projects. Each project is a product of creativity, teamwork, and learning. Browse the repositories to explore what students have built!

## Getting Started

If you participated in a SchoolHackDay event and want to submit your project:

1. **Create a repository** under this organisation (or ask an organiser to set one up for you).
2. **Add a README** to your project repository describing what it does, how to run it, and who worked on it.
3. **Push your code** and share the link with your fellow hackers.

## Contributing

We welcome contributions from all students! Whether you're fixing a bug, adding a feature, or improving documentation, here's how to get involved:

1. **Fork** the repository you'd like to contribute to.
2. **Create a branch** for your change (`git checkout -b my-feature`).
3. **Commit** your changes with a clear message.
4. **Open a Pull Request** and describe what you've done.

## Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone. Please:

- Be respectful and kind to your fellow students and organisers.
- Give credit where it's due — acknowledge teammates and any resources you used.
- Have fun and keep learning!
